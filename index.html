Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about our manpower, staffing, and payroll outsourcing services.

What manpower and staffing services does Pushpa Hireedge Services provide?

We provide construction manpower, industrial manpower, hospitality staffing, healthcare manpower, telecalling services, driver services, housekeeping staff, warehouse & helper staff, security services, and payroll outsourcing — all delivered across India.

Which cities and regions does Pushpa Hireedge Services operate in?

We are headquartered in Dhar, Madhya Pradesh, and supply verified manpower and staffing solutions to businesses across India, working with clients across multiple states and sectors.

How quickly can you fulfil a bulk manpower requirement?

Depending on the role, skill level, and location, we typically shortlist and deploy verified, job-ready candidates within a few days to two weeks of receiving your requirement.

Do you handle payroll and statutory compliance?

Yes. We offer end-to-end payroll outsourcing, including PF/ESI statutory compliance and employee benefits administration, so you can focus entirely on your core business.

How can job seekers register with Pushpa Hireedge Services?

Job seekers can register directly through our Contact section below, or reach out via phone or email, to get matched with openings that fit their skills, location, and career goals.
